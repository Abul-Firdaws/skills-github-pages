I had my first major intercity travel in 2013 when I had admission to study at the university. Then, I did not know Kumasi, the capital of the Ashanti Region.
Before using the Kumasi route, I had used the Techiman-Nkronza-Ejura-Mampong roads to get to campus. I only got to use the Kumasi highway after my first semester exam.
